📌 Article Summary

What to Carry Forward

AI skills are appearing across all major tech roles — not just AI/ML positions. Software engineers, DevOps engineers, security analysts, and data scientists are all expected to work fluently with AI tools as part of their standard workflow.

What employers consistently ask for: effective AI tool usage with judgment about output quality. Prompt engineering, AI output verification, and knowing when human oversight is needed are differentiators across every role.

The emerging differentiator: AI governance, responsible AI practices, and AI risk assessment are increasingly valued — especially at larger organizations subject to regulatory scrutiny. These skills, which this certification develops, are relatively rare and increasingly in demand.
