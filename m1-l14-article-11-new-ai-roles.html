What to Carry Forward

New roles emerging from AI: AI/LLM Engineer (building with foundation models), Prompt Engineer (optimizing AI interactions), AI Product Manager (product strategy for AI features), AI Safety Researcher (ensuring safe model behavior), AI Governance Specialist (compliance and risk). All are in high demand and short supply.

The most accessible entry from a general tech background: AI Engineer (LLM integration). The skills transfer directly from software engineering, and the demand is enormous as every company builds LLM-powered features.

The skills to build now: LLM API integration, evaluation framework design, prompt engineering, RAG systems, and AI governance basics. These appear across multiple new role profiles and represent durable capability regardless of which specific role you pursue.
